Movie Reviews

Inception

Genre: Sci-Fi, Action

Summary: A thief enters people's dreams to steal secrets and plant ideas in their minds.

Review: A mind-bending thriller by Christopher Nolan, blending spectacular visuals with a deep philosophical plot.

The Shawshank Redemption

Genre: Drama

Summary: The story of a banker sentenced to life who never gives up hope while forming friendships in prison.

Review: An emotionally powerful and inspiring film with stellar performances and a satisfying ending.

Interstellar

Genre: Sci-Fi, Drama

Summary: Astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.

Review: A visually stunning and thought-provoking journey through time, space, and human emotion.

The Dark Knight

Genre: Action, Crime

Summary: Batman faces the chaos brought by Joker in a battle for Gotham’s soul.

Review: An intense, gritty superhero film elevated by Heath Ledger’s iconic Joker performance.

3 Idiots

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Summary: A journey of three engineering students questioning the educational system.
