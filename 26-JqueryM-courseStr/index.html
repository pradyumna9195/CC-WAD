PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Mathematics-I: Fundamentals of calculus and linear algebra.
  • Basic Electrical Engineering: Concepts of circuits and machines.
  • Engineering Physics: Modern physics for engineers.
  • Engineering Chemistry: Chemical principles for materials and environment.
  • Programming in C: Introduction to structured programming.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures: Stacks, queues, trees, and graphs.
  • Digital Electronics: Logic circuits and number systems.
  • Computer Organization: CPU, memory, and instruction cycles.
  • Discrete Mathematics: Logic, sets, and relations.
  • Object-Oriented Programming (Java): Classes, objects, and inheritance.

Third Year (TE)

  • Database Management Systems: SQL, normalization, and transactions.
  • Operating Systems: Process, memory, and file management.
  • Theory of Computation: Automata and complexity theory.
  • Software Engineering: SDLC, UML, and project management.
  • Web Technologies: HTML, CSS, JS, and backend concepts.

Final Year (BE)

  • Machine Learning: Supervised and unsupervised algorithms.
  • Cloud Computing: Virtualization, IaaS, PaaS, SaaS.
  • Information and Cyber Security: Cryptography and network security.
  • Electives: Choose from AI, IoT, Blockchain, etc.
  • Project Work: Full-year capstone project.
